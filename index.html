How KnitFix works

No complicated forms. Just a quick photo, a price, and a clear timeline. Here’s what happens from the moment you message me to the moment your knit is back in your wardrobe.

Step 1

Send a photo

WhatsApp or email a couple of photos of the damage (front, inside, and a zoomed out shot). I’ll confirm if it’s repairable and give you a price estimate.

Free assessment No obligation
Step 2

Ship or drop off

Post your knit to the studio or bring it in person. I log it, match yarn as closely as possible and plan the repair so it feels good against the skin.

Tracked postage Careful handling
Step 3

Repair & return

I repair the damaged area, steam-finish the knit and send it back within the agreed timeframe – usually 7 days once it arrives at the studio.

7-day turnaround Ready to rewear
